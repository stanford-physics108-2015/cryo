Specifications

 
 
Standard curve1 102 and 202: 0.05 K to 40 K; 103: 1.4 K to 40 K
 
Recommended excitation2RX-102 and RX-202 : 20 µV (0.05 K to 0.1 K); 63 µV (0.1 K to 1.2 K); 10 mV or less for T > 1 K. RX-103: 10 mV or less for T > 1 K.
 
Dissipation at recommended excitation
102 and 202: 7.5 × 10–8 W at 4.2 K;
103: 3.2 × 10–9 W at 1.4 K, 5.5 × 10–9 W at 4.2 K, 9.6 × 10–9 W at 77 K
 
Thermal response time 0.5 s at 4.2 K, 2.5 s at 77 K
 
Radiation effects Recommended – Click here for more information
 
Magnetic field Recommended – Click here for more information

3102B not recommended for use in magnetic fields
