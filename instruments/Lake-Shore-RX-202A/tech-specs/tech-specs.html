Specifications

 
 
Standard curve1 102 and 202: 0.05 K to 40 K; 103: 1.4 K to 40 K
 
Recommended excitation2RX-102 and RX-202 : 20 V (0.05 K to 0.1 K); 63 V (0.1 K to 1.2 K); 10 mV or less for T > 1 K. RX-103: 10 mV or less for T > 1 K.
 
Dissipation at recommended excitation
102 and 202: 7.5  108 W at 4.2 K;
103: 3.2  109 W at 1.4 K, 5.5  109 W at 4.2 K, 9.6  109 W at 77 K
 
Thermal response time 0.5 s at 4.2 K, 2.5 s at 77 K
